## Website Goal

The website should feel modern, trustworthy, premium, and technical.

The goal is not to look like a traditional corporate website.

Reference style:

* Vercel
* Stripe
* Linear
* Resend

Keywords:

* Modern
* Clean
* Enterprise
* Premium
* Trustworthy
* Technical

## Design Principles

* Clean layouts
* Strong typography
* Large spacing
* Minimal visual noise
* Clear hierarchy
* Subtle animations
* Mobile first
* Fast loading

## Rules

* Do not introduce unnecessary complexity.
* Prefer simple and elegant solutions.
* Keep sections focused and easy to scan.
* Prioritize readability over decoration.
* Maintain performance and responsiveness.
* Avoid excessive gradients and flashy effects.
